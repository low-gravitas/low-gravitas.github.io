*Last updated: June 29, 2026*

This Privacy Policy applies to **You Have a Thing**, a macOS app by Low Gravitas.

## The short version

You Have a Thing runs **entirely on your Mac**. It does **not** collect,
transmit, store off-device, or sell any personal information. There are no
accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. We never see
your data, because it never leaves your computer.

## Calendar access

The app uses macOS Calendar access (EventKit) to read your upcoming events so it
can show them in the menu bar and remind you before they start. This happens
**locally on your device**. Your events, their titles, attendees, and links are
**never sent anywhere** — not to us, not to anyone. You can grant or revoke
calendar access at any time in **System Settings → Privacy & Security →
Calendars**.

## What's stored, and where

Your preferences — settings, notification thresholds, themes — are stored
locally on your Mac (in standard macOS preferences). They are not synced to a
cloud service by the app and are not transmitted anywhere.

## Network use

The app makes no network connections of its own. When you click **Join** on a
meeting, the app asks macOS to open that meeting's link in your default web
browser; from that point your browser and the meeting provider handle the
connection, under their own privacy policies.

## No tracking, no third parties

You Have a Thing contains **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party
SDKs**. It does not track you across apps or websites.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from
anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date above.
